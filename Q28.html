Maggi Noodles Recipes

INGREDINETS

for masaledar maggi:

  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 clove garlic (finely chopped)
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • ½ carrot (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp capsicum (chopped)
  • ½ tomato (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp sweet corn
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 pack maggi noodles
  • 1 pack tastemaker
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Steps to make masaledar masala maggi :

  1. firstly, in pan heat 2 tsp oil and saute 2 clove garlic and ½ onion.
  2. add ½ carrot, 2 tbsp capsicum, ½ tomato and 2 tbsp sweet corn.
  3. saute until the vegetables turn crunchy.
  4. further add ¼ turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp salt and saute until the spices turn aromatic.
  5. add 1 cup water and get to a boil.
  6. now bread 1 pack maggi noodles roughly and add 1 pack tastemaker.
  7. mix well and boil for 2 minutes or until the noodles are cooked well.
  8. finally, add 2 tbsp coriander and enjoy masaledar maggi.
