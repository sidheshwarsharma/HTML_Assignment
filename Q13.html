A TIME TO KILL

BY JOHN GRISHAM
This one is from 3:3 in the Ecclesiastes, again part of the Old Testament.The anonymous author is a King of Jerusalem who relates and analyses events in 
his own life.This has resonated strongly with a lot of people: Abraham Lincoln quoted Ecclesiastes when addressing Congress in 1862, and
the novelist Thomas Wolfe called it ‘the greatest single piece of writing I have ever known.
’ Grisham’s 1989 title is taken from the line that [To every thing there is a season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven:] 
A time to kill, and a time to heal; a time to break down, and a time to build up…
